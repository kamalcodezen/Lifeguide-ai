# Project Vision: LifeGuide AI

**AI-Powered Career & Learning Copilot**

## Executive Summary

LifeGuide AI is an AI-powered platform designed to guide students, job seekers, and career changers through the complex process of learning, skill-gap analysis, portfolio building, and career readiness. It bridges the gap between academic education/self-guided tutorials and real-world employment demands.

## Core Pillars

1. **Interactive Profiling:** Understanding current user skill levels, learning style, and career goals.
2. **Dynamic Learning Paths:** Curating personalized, adaptive learning schedules mapping back to actual job market needs.
3. **Targeted Portfolio Building:** Prompting users to build custom, non-trivial projects to prove competence.
4. **Validation & Coach:** Providing interview simulators and resume match optimization to maximize job placement odds.
